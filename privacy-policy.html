Privacy Policy – Mealnest

Last updated: May 2026

Mealnest is a meal planner, recipe book, shopping list, and family task management app. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled.

What data we collect

Mealnest stores data exclusively on your device (browser localStorage):

  • Recipes you create or import
  • Meal plans and shopping lists
  • Tasks and reminders
  • App settings (theme, language, preferences)

If you sign in with Google, the app stores your name, email, and profile picture solely for display purposes in the app settings.

Where data is stored

All data is stored locally in your browser. If you create a family code, your meal plan and shopping list are shared with family members via Firebase Realtime Database (Google Cloud). If you use AI features, your requests are sent via Cloudflare Worker to the DeepSeek API. No personal data is stored on third-party servers.

What we do NOT collect

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No cookies
  • No data sold to third parties
  • No passwords — login is exclusively via Google

Your rights

All data is stored on your device. You can delete it anytime via Settings → Data → Delete all data.
